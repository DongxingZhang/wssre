Special Report - China: Preview of CPC Party Congress (Zhiwei Zhang)

The Communist Party of China will convene its 19th National Congress(hereafter the Party Congress) on Oct 18. In this report we discuss what weexpect from this important political event. The leadership reshuffle in China is aprocess that will last from the Party Congress in Oct to the National People'sCongress (NPC) in Mar 2018. There are three important events to watch in thisprocess: the Party Congress, the central economic working conference (CEWC)in December, and the NPC. (Zhiwei Zhang ¨C 852 2203 8308)
