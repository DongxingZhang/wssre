HSUEH ON OIL - Decision time again

Michael Hsueh: OPEC's effort has been a moderate success. If there is anunbiased way to measure OPEC's struggle to support the market while alsoconstraining emergent US supply, it is shown in the price performance ofthe current cycle versus previous supply tightening cycles. Since implementingsupply restraint in January 2017, Brent front month prices have risen by 12%. Thisstands in the middle of the pack relative to previous cycles.
