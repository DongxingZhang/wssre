The Communist Party of China will convene its 19th National Congress (hereafterthe Party Congress) on Oct 18. In this report we discuss what we expect from thisimportant political event.

The Party Congress is the beginning of a six-month process

The leadership reshuffle in China is a process that will last from the PartyCongress in Oct to the National People's Congress (NPC) in Mar 2018. There arethree important events to watch in this process: the Party Congress, the centraleconomic working conference (CEWC) in December, and the NPC.
