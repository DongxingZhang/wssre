Meager auto sales growth for 2017is almost for certain

Chinese passenger vehicle (PV) wholesale volume remained flattish in November,in line with our expectation, with still a healthy inventory situation. We believethere will be a limited variation to our 2.0% 2017China PV sales growth forecast,even considering potential last-minute purchases before the year-end expiry of taxcut stimulus. Our preferred OEM names are BAIC Motor and Brilliance on highercertainty in their key brands' 2018E sales strength.

No November YoY wholesale growth on high base

Based on China Association of Automobile Manufacturers' (CAAM) Novembersales data, SUV sales were up 8.9% YoY (accounting for 42.8% of China PV sales).As local brands' November SUV sales made up for 63.5% of total SUV sales,overall local brand sales recorded a 5.0% YoY increase. Although the sub-1.6L PVsegment recorded 2.0% YoY sales volume drop in November, there was a 10.0%MoM sales increase, implying a continuation of last-minute purchase demand,in our view. Meanwhile, above-1.6L PV wholesale sales improved 4.8% YoY inNovember, on our estimates, probably driven by decent premium brand salesamid the Chinese consumption upgrade.

Inventory expectation staying optimistic and easing pricing pressure

The China Automobile Dealers Association (CADA) reported that the Octoberdealer inventory index eased MoM to 1.33months, and its November inventoryalert index is at 49.8%, implying dealers' continual optimism. A healthy demandsupplysituation is also reflected in slightly lower discounts in recent months.Although the China Passenger Car Association (CPCA) and WAYS forecast thatDecember mainstream PV (i.e., excluding mini-car) wholesale volume will grow6.4%, we are less upbeat considering the high December 2016sales base.

We prefer premium auto and Korean brands' recovery themes

We use P/E to value auto OEMs and base our target prices for auto dealers onDCF analysis. Our OEM preferred names are BAIC Motor and Brilliance, givenstrong sales at their premium JV brands, while BAIC Motor will also benefit fromits Hyundai JV recovery.
