Valuation and risks.

We have a Buy recommendation on both companies due to the upside fromtheir respective new retail strategies. Our primary valuation methodology is DCF,employing a WACC of 9.5% and a terminal growth rate of 2.0%, which is withinthe range that we use for Chinese consumer companies (1.0-2.5%). We use theDeutsche Bank China COE of 9.5%, which is in line with other retailers in China.

We use a beta of 1.0. Downside risks: 1) weak SSSG; 2) slower-than-expectedexpansion plans; 3) improvement in GPM failing to offset an opex increase; and4) competition from e-commerce and offline peers.
