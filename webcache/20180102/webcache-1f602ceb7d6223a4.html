A-Share Market Color: Opportunities to remain limited to certain sectors in the absence ofmarket-wide strength; stick to earnings-focused stock selection

Several fund managers that we talked to believe that we will continue to see a slow bull market in2018and that investment opportunities will remain limited to a few certain sectors whereas acrossthe-board share price strength is unlikely to be seen. Blue-chip stocks and emerging sectors werementioned by quite a few fund managers as preferred investment targets. Many believe that anearnings-focused stock selection strategy will remain crucial next year amid such a marketenvironment. In addition, infrastructure investment and other economic data are cited by some of thefund managers as key to watch in 1Q18.
