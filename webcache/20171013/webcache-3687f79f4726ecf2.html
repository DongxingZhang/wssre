Genuine corporate governance change in Japan would be a major event forinvestors. Japan has a long road to travel to reach global standards. The goodnews is that Japan has begun the journey, and total pay-out ratios have risen toaround 50%. The bad news is that progress is hard to identify in the corporateefficiency metrics of ROA and ROE. Investors are receiving a higher share of astill relatively small cake. This leaves Japan continuing to lag global peers.
